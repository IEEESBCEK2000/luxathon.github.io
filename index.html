LUXATHON 3.0

Luxathon is world's first 24-Hour university level electrical illumination electrical hackathon conducted by college of Engineering Karunagappally within 24 hours the participants has to design and develop an illumination system according to the specified theme and requirements by using given equipment in the most efficient way and according to standards

What to do?

Register

Register as a team of 4-5 members.Work together as team.Win as team

Prepare

All necessary equipments will be provided to the participants except arduino or raspberry pi board that should be brought by each team or can be hired from the venue. Also each team should carry one laptop.

Participate

A mentoring session explaining the bare minimum concepts will be provided by experts so that anyone with basic electrical knowledge can participate in the hackathon.

Compete

The competition will be judged and mentored by a panel of experts from electrical academic and industrial fileds.
